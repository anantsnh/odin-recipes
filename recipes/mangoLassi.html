Mango Lassi Recipe

Description

Mango lassi is a delicious creamy drink consisting of mango, yogurt, and milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 (15.25 ounce) cans mango pulp, or mango slices with juice
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Pour mangos, yogurt, milk, and ice into the bowl of a blender. Blend until smooth.
